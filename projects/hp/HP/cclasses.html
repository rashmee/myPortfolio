Core Classes

All first year student at Hogwarts have to take the 7 core subjects. In the first year they are taught at the entry level to help better accustom them to what they should expect in the fututre and also to help make choice in their later career.

Every class is taken with the peers of your house and peers from your house and also one other house. Two houses of the same year are taught at the same time. This will differ from class to class.

The core classes are:

  • Astronomy
  • Charms
  • Defence Against the Dark Arts
  • Flying
  • Herbology
  • History of Magic
  • Potions
  • Transfiguration

Other Requirements for the class

    Uniform

  • Three sets of plain, black work robes
  • One plain black pointed hat for day wear
  • One pair of dragon hide or similar protective gloves
  • One black with silver fastingings winter cloak


    Other Equipment

  • 1 Wand
  • 1 Cauldron (gold but preferabaly pewter, standard size 2)
  • 1 set of glass or crystal phials
  • 1 telescope
  • 1 set of brass scales
  • Students may also bring either a:
    • cat
    • toad
    • owl
