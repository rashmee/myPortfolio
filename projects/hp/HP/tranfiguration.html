Information about the Tranfiguration class:

About the Class The student in this class learns how to tranform the appearance of an object. You start with turning water to wine and go on to turning yourself into an animal or an animagus. This class is madatory from years one through five.
Professor Minerva McGonagall Professor McGonagall is one of the most respected and most experienced teacher there is at Hogwarts. She is also the Deputy Headmistress. Her animagus is a tabby cat.
Class Details Class Location This class meets in class 99. This class usually meets various times in the week. Please refer to schedule on the first day of class.
Required Material
  • A Beginner's Guide to Transfiguration by Emeric Switch
  • Intermediate Transfiguration
  • A Guide to Advanced Transfiguration
Wand All the books can be purchased at Flourish and Blotts Bookstore in Diagon Alley.
