Extra-Curricular Subjects

These are classes that are chosen as per the student's interests. These classes do not receive any credit. These classes only meet when the students are free from classes and SHOULD NOT interfere with the student's studies.

If any new clubs or activities are in formation, they need to be registered.

The Extra-Curricular Activities active at present:

Ancient Studies

   --> This class mainly focuses on magic taught in the ancient times. You will be taught old Egyptian spells.

   --> This class meets at the classroom on the sixth floor.

Art

   --> This class focuses on wizard art and is only available to third year students and up.

   --> This class meets at the classroom on the fifth floor.

Earth Magic

   --> This class focuses on magic that is present within the earth. It requires getting your hands dirty.

   --> This class meets at the grounds within the walla of the school.

Muggle Art

   --> This is a non-magic study. Students focus on the muggle artefacts and paintings.

   --> This class is meets with Professor Charity Babbage inside a classroom on the fifth floor.

Hogwarts Orchestra

   --> This is a class that focuses on learning magical music. This is only available to the students for students, third year and up.

   --> This class meets in the music classroom.

Muggle Music

   --> This is a non-magic study. Students learn some muggle music. This class is only available to the students for students, third year and up.

   --> This class is taught by the Charms professor, Filius Flitwick in the classroom on the fifth floor.

Ghoul Studies

   --> This class focuses on the ghouls, ghosts and the poltergeists of the magical world.

   --> This class meets at the classroom on the Serpentine Corridor.

Magical Theory

   --> This class focuses on the theoretical aspect of magic.

Xylomancy

   --> This is a section of divination that works with twigs. They meet at the North Tower.

Frog Choir

   --> This is a kind of music for which training in music is given to the singer along with their toad.

   --> This class is taught by the Charms professor, Filius Flitwick in the Great Hall.
