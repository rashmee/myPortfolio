Information about the Divination class:

About the Class The student in this class learns about telling the future and also making prophecies. Student will learn to read tea leaves and orbs. This class is an elective from third year on.
Professor Seeing the future is tricky business. It seemed like a good idea to have a second teacher as a second opinion.
