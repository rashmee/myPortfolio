ABOUT DOMESTIC VIOLENCE


What is domestic violence?
Domestic violence constitutes a broad range of behaviors occurring along a continuum from voyeurism to rape, and usually happens over an extended period of time.

How common is it?
Domestic violence is occurring in pandemic proportions and causes grave physical and psychological trauma, along with social havoc

It is difficult to know the true extent of domestic violence. Most domestic violence is never reported (due to shame, fear, stigma, manipulation, and other factors).Â 
What are the consequences?
We all privately or publicly know survivors who have not only survived, but also thrived; it has taken work for them to get there. Often extremely severe consequences include:

  • Depression, anxiety, PTSD, and other psychological problems
  • Anti-social behaviors
  • Decreased school performance and delinquency
  • Substance abuse
  • Promiscuity
  • Teen pregnancy
  • Sexually transmitted infections and diseases, including HIV
  • Prostitution
  • Homicide and suicide
  • Incarceration
  • Chronic disease
