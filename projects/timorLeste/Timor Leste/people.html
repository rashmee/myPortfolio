Facts about the people from Timor-Leste

  • The citizens of Timor-Leste are called Timorese.
  • The three ethnic groups are Papuan, Austronesian, small Chinese minority.
  • Their official language is Tetum and due to high influence of Portugal many of the Timorese people speak Portuguese. Indonesian and english also are prominently spoken in this region.
  • About 98% of the population is Roman-Catholic, 1% of the population is Muslim, 1% of them are Protestants.
  • The population growth rate us 2.47%.
  • The birth rate is 34.85 births/1,000 and the death rate is 6.28-deaths/1,000 population.
